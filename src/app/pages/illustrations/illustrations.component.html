Drawing has been a passion of mine ever since I was old enough to pick up a pencil. My grandma used to tell stories of keeping me busy during warm afternoons with nothing but a pencil and paper. Throughout the years I have done portrait commissions and art pieces for friends and acquaintances. I love to draw faces, their subtle complexities are something I work hard to capture as I practice and improve.

Currently most of my work is done on Procreate, on an iPad, but I have had plenty of practice using Photoshop and Illustrator as well.
